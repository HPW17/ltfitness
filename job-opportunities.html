Job Opportunities

Fitness Trainer

Description: Design and implement personalized fitness programs, conduct training sessions, and motivate clients to achieve their fitness goals.

Nutrition Specialist

Description: Provide nutrition counseling, create customized meal plans, and educate clients on the importance of a balanced diet for optimal health and fitness.

Front Desk Coordinator

Description: Manage front desk operations, greet members, handle inquiries, and ensure a positive customer experience at the fitness facility.

Group Fitness Instructor

Description: Lead energetic group fitness classes, design workout routines, and create a motivating environment for participants.

Marketing Coordinator

Description: Develop and implement marketing strategies, manage social media presence, and collaborate with the team to promote fitness programs and events.

Personal Wellness Coach

Description: Provide one-on-one coaching, support clients in setting and achieving wellness goals, and address both physical and mental aspects of health.

Maintenance Technician

Description: Ensure proper maintenance of fitness equipment, facilities, and infrastructure to create a safe and clean environment for members.

Health and Safety Officer

Description: Implement health and safety protocols, conduct regular inspections, and educate staff and members on maintaining a secure fitness environment.

Membership Sales Representative

Description: Drive membership sales, conduct tours for potential clients, and assist in developing promotional strategies to attract new members.

Event Coordinator

Description: Plan and execute fitness events, workshops, and challenges, coordinating logistics and ensuring a seamless experience for participants.
