MEET
THE STAFF

Our LT Staff are among the best and are dedicated to promoting a healthy lifestyle for adults and kids of all ages.

Founder & Director: Latoya

Latoya wasn't born with privilege, but with worn sneakers and a fierce spirit. Basketball became her escape, a court her community. Witnessing health struggles in her adulthood, she knew action was needed. Bootstrapping her passion, Latoya created LT Fitness, a non-profit pulsing with fitness classes and laughter. Not a CEO, but a coach in sneakers, Latoya led by example, understanding well-being wasn't a luxury, but a tapestry woven from movement, education, and community. Today, LT Fitness thrives, a testament to Latoya's belief that everyone deserves a healthy life, proving change sometimes starts with worn sneakers and a fierce spirit.

Head Fitness Trainter: James

Forget intimidating gyms, James thrives at LT Fitness, a non-profit making fitness accessible to all. No sculpted abs mantra here, it's "joyful movement for every body." His classes are a kaleidoscope of movement, a celebration of community, not just reps. You won't find him barking orders, but high-fiving, encouraging, and ensuring everyone feels valued. Beyond the gym, he volunteers, mentors, and teaches healthy cooking. So, if you want a trainer who prioritizes smiles and believes movement is a celebration, find James at LT Fitness. He'll show you fitness can be fun, inclusive, and a powerful tool for a healthier you.

Program Director: Sarah

Sarah's not a spreadsheet queen, she's the heartbeat of LT Fitness. As Program Director, she crafts experiences, not just workouts. Forget intimidating gyms, think laughter-filled yoga, community dance-offs, and playful strength training. Sarah knows well-being thrives beyond the gym, and her passion extends far beyond the physical. So, if you want a program director who prioritizes smiles and believes movement is a celebration, find Sarah at LT Fitness. She'll show you fitness can be fun, inclusive, and a powerful tool for your best self.

Volunteer Coordinator: Seth

Seth isn't your typical suit-and-tie coordinator. At LT Fitness, a non-profit where community thrives through sweat and smiles, he ignites the passion of volunteers. Once an introverted techie, Seth discovered connection's power and now fuels his mission to empower everyone, regardless of background. Forget rigid structures, Seth sees volunteers as co-creators, matching their skills to lead classes, mentor youth, or offer tech support. He believes well-being is a tapestry woven by many hands, and his passion goes beyond scheduling. Beyond coordination, he champions LT Fitness's inclusivity efforts and even started a volunteer recognition program. He'll inspire you to connect, contribute, and discover your vibrant potential within the community.

We believe in promoting a healthy lifestyle for all ages.
