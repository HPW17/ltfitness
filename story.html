LT FITNESS & PERFORMANCE LLC’S FOUNDING STORY

LT Fitness was founded in 2015 as Latoya Jones saw the need in her community for more health and wellness programs. With a dream of building an inclusive and accessible wellness organization, she enlisted the help of friends and family to help create her vision. Fast forward almost 10 years and LT Fitness has grown into a multi-tiered organization with programs for kids and adults. And... they’re just getting started.

MISSION, VISION & VALUES

Mission: The mission of LT Fitness is to empower all individuals to thrive through accessible, inclusive, and joyful fitness and healthy lifestyle programs.

Vision: A world where everyone has the opportunity and support to live a healthy and fulfilling life.

Values:

  • Accessibility: We believe that everyone, regardless of background, ability, or circumstance, deserves access to the tools and resources needed to live a healthy life.
  • Joy: We believe that movement and healthy living should be enjoyable and sustainable, sparking an intrinsic desire for lifelong well-being.
  • Empowerment: We equip individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to make informed choices about
  • Collaboration: We work in partnership with individuals, communities, and organizations to create a ripple effect of positive health change.

POSITIVE IMPACT

LT Fitness has...
  • Worked with over 900 children
  • Trained over 300 adults
  • Offered over 100 fitness classes
  • Partnered with over 50 communities
  • Created nutritional resources
  • Implemented a volunteer program
  • Provides individualized training programs
  • Fostered a sense of love for wellness

We believe in promoting a healthy lifestyle for all ages.
